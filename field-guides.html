Practical ways to use VellumGuard.

Short, honest guides for teams adding post-quantum application-layer encryption to real workflows. Each guide covers the problem, an example, where VellumGuard fits, and what it does not replace.

Healthcare and research records

Protect clinical, research, and patient-adjacent records that may remain sensitive for 10 or more years. VellumGuard can help teams test application-layer encryption and post-quantum migration paths without replacing every system at once.

Not currently HIPAA compliant. No BAA currently available.

CMMC-adjacent contractor workflows

For defense contractors, VellumGuard can support stronger protection and auditability for sensitive application data. It does not make an organization CMMC compliant by itself, but it can help demonstrate stronger encryption, key-management discipline, and operational evidence as part of a broader security program.
