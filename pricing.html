Questions, answered

The fine print, in plain language.

What counts as an operation?

One encrypt or decrypt call, regardless of payload size up to 10 MB. Creating, rotating, and revoking identities is always free.

What happens if I exceed my tier?

Free rejects extra requests with a clear error — never a surprise bill. Paid tiers soft-cap and email you to upgrade before anything breaks.

Is there a startup discount?

Yes — 50% off Business for twelve months if you've raised under $5M and are pre-Series B. Write to startups@vellumguard.com.

Can I avoid lock-in?

The SDK is open-source and the ciphertext format is public with reference vectors. Your data stays decryptable with any conforming implementation.
