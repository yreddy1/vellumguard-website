Questions, answered

The fine print, in plain language.

What counts as an operation?

One encrypt or decrypt call, regardless of payload size up to 10 MB. Creating and revoking identities is always free.

What happens if I exceed my tier?

Free rejects extra requests with a clear error — never a surprise bill. Paid tiers soft-cap and notify you to upgrade before anything breaks.

Is there a startup discount?

Possibly — contact beta@vellumguard.com to discuss your situation. No commitments made during beta without a written agreement.

Can I avoid lock-in?

The SDK is open-source and the ciphertext format is public with reference vectors. Your data stays decryptable with any conforming implementation.
